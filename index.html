Controls

  • D - walking to the right
  • D + D - running to the right
  • A - walking to the left
  • A + A - running to the left
  • W (idle) - jumping
  • W (walking) - walking and jumping
  • W (running) - running and jumping
  • S - teleporting (only works when the player is in idle state)
  • I - attacking
  • O - throwing knives
  • P - special attack
